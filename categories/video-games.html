Getting Started with Shop2not for Video Games

Setting up Shop2not for your video game business is straightforward:

  1. Install the Extension: Add Shop2not to your Chrome browser from the Chrome Web Store
  2. Navigate to Your Shopify Admin: Go to your Shopify products page
  3. Select Your Video Games: Choose the games you want to export to Whatnot
  4. Export with One Click: Click the Shop2not button and download your Whatnot-ready CSV
  5. Upload to Whatnot: Import the CSV file directly into your Whatnot seller dashboard
