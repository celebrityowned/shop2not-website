Tips for Success

  • Use high-quality, well-lit photos of each Funko Pop (front, back, box condition).
  • Include details like exclusives, limited editions, and box condition in your descriptions.
  • Set competitive starting prices to encourage bidding.
  • Engage with buyers during live sales to build loyalty.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Missing or inaccurate product details (e.g., edition, number, exclusivity)
  • Poor quality or missing images
  • Incorrect category selection on Whatnot
  • Not updating inventory after sales
