Tips for Success Selling Graded Sports Cards on Whatnot

  • Engage with the Community: Participate in live chats, answer questions, and build relationships with repeat buyers.
  • Promote Your Sales: Share your Whatnot streams and listings on social media and in relevant sports card groups to drive more traffic.
