How Shopify stores can sell Vintage Video Games on Whatnot

Vintage video games are highly sought after by collectors and nostalgic gamers alike. If you have a Shopify store with classic games, consoles, or accessories, you can tap into a passionate community by selling on Whatnot. This guide will help you get started and succeed using Shop2not.

Why Sell Vintage Video Games on Whatnot?

  • Strong demand from collectors and retro gaming fans
  • Live auctions create excitement and competitive bidding
  • Easy inventory export with Shop2not

How to List Vintage Video Games on Whatnot from Shopify

  1. Organize your inventory in Shopify, including game title, platform, condition, and completeness.
  2. Use Shop2not to export your vintage game products to a Whatnot-ready CSV file.
  3. Import the CSV into Whatnot and review your listings for accuracy.
  4. Host live sales or auctions to connect with retro gaming enthusiasts!

Tips for Success

  • Use clear, high-resolution photos of each game, box, and manual.
  • Include details like platform, region, condition (CIB, loose, sealed), and any defects.
  • Mention if games are tested and working.
  • Engage with buyers during live streams to build trust and excitement.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Missing or inaccurate game details
  • Poor quality or missing images
  • Incorrect category selection on Whatnot
  • Not updating inventory after sales
