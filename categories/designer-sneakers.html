How to List Designer Sneakers on Whatnot from Shopify

  2. Use Shop2not to export your sneaker products to a Whatnot-ready CSV file.

Tips for Success

  • Use crisp, high-resolution photos of each sneaker (multiple angles).
  • Include details like brand, model, size, colorway, and condition.
  • Mention any authentication or original packaging.
  • Engage with buyers during live sales to build hype.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Missing or inaccurate sneaker details
  • Poor quality or missing images
  • Incorrect category selection on Whatnot
  • Not updating inventory after sales
