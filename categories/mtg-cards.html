How Shopify stores can sell Magic: The Gathering Cards on Whatnot

Magic: The Gathering (MTG) cards are a staple of the trading card world, with a passionate community of collectors and players. If you run a Shopify store with MTG singles, sealed products, or accessories, you can reach a wider audience by selling on Whatnot. This guide shows you how to get started and maximize your sales using Shop2not.

Why Sell MTG Cards on Whatnot?

  • Large, engaged community of TCG buyers
  • Live auctions drive excitement and higher prices
  • Easy inventory export with Shop2not

How to List MTG Cards on Whatnot from Shopify

  1. Organize your MTG inventory in Shopify, including card name, set, condition, and rarity.
  2. Use Shop2not to export your MTG products to a Whatnot-ready CSV file.
  3. Import the CSV into Whatnot and review your listings for accuracy.
  4. Host live breaks, auctions, or sales to connect with MTG fans!

Tips for Success

  • Use clear, high-resolution scans or photos of each card (front and back).
  • Include details like set, rarity, condition (NM, LP, etc.), and any foiling.
  • Mention if cards are graded or sealed.
  • Engage with buyers during live streams to build trust and excitement.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Missing or inaccurate card details
  • Poor quality or missing images
  • Incorrect category selection on Whatnot
  • Not updating inventory after sales
